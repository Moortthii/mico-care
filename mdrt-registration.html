Registration

Registration & Training

  • Complete the required Workshop Registration Form and upload to the portal or email to mdrt2communications@gmail.com, along with your highest level of qualification.
  • Proof of teacher training certification or equivalent qualification must be submitted for verification before training is administered.
  • Payment of the workshop registration fee must be made on or before the specified time and proof of payment submitted. Payments can be made in person at the Mico CARE Centre, through direct deposit or wire transfer.
  • The prescribed shipping and handling fee (as below) for the MDRT-II test manual must be paid along with the registration fee where this is the selected mode of delivery. Participants will have the option of collecting the manual in person at the Mico CARE Centre (Kingston) as well.
