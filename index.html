About Us

The Mico University College CARE Centre was established in 1980 as a Centre of Excellence, dedicated to child assessment and research in education. It was formed through a partnership between the Netherlands Government and the Government of Jamaica.

  • Founded in 1980 as a specialized education centre.
  • A collaboration between Jamaica and the Netherlands.
  • Focused on child assessment and educational research.
  • Supported by the Netherlands (building & equipment) and Jamaica (land).

Our Services

Empowering Every Learner:

The Mico CARE Centre supports diverse learners through services such as diagnosing learning challenges, guiding gifted students, and providing remedial interventions. It also conducts special education research, trains teachers, facilitates clinical practice for tertiary students, and develops culturally relevant assessment tools for the Caribbean.

Partnerships

In March 2023 The Mico University College and Eastern Illinois University (EIU) in the United States, signed a memorandum of understanding signalling their commitment to collaborative activities between both institutions. Among the initial partnerships was the speech therapy project. This project is a unique initiative which involves graduate students in the speech therapy programme at EIU providing weekly speech therapy sessions, through video conferencing, to clients at Mico CARE Centre under the supervision of EIU faculty.

In 2024 the partnership extended to a study abroad programme, where EIU students spend a week at Mico CARE Centre, St Ann, providing in-person therapy to clients, while their professors conduct speech evaluations and guide treatment plans. This project has allowed more clients access to speech therapy and has also provided rich cultural immersion for EIU students while honing their skills in teletherapy.
